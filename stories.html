Bio

Maurice was born in Cardiff, though left in his early ears to work for fun fairs in London. He'd spend the next few years working in construction around the country before returning to Cardiff "I came back to Cardiff for good when I was 30, got a job on buildings, then they let me go"

Unfortunately, Maurice was unlucky enough to develop emphysema, without a regular source of income he fell on hard times. With a lit bit of support from the big issue, and some help from local housing organisations Maurice has managed to move out of temporary accommodation

These days you can see him Cardiff city centre, selling a copy of the Issue, he has a regular shift every day from 9 to 4.
